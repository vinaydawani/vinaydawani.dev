SpotiViz

A visual representation of your music taste.

SpotiViz is a small data visualization webapp made by Vinay Dawani for Design 5505 class at The Ohio State University. This is a simple app which fetches data from your Spotify account and displays various properties for the tracks like "speechiness" or "loudness". This app uses Spotify API to fetch for data.
SpotiViz gets your top 5 tracks and compares their properties and popularity. It also goes on to get all your saved tracks with their properties and compares "energy" and "danceability" to "valence". Another feature is that this app generates a second scatter chart of "energy" with respect to "acousticness". together.
